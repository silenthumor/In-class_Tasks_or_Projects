Details:

For the navigation, feel free to be creative and use your own colors and fonts etc. If you wish to duplicate the navigation below I used the following:

  • Google Anton font
  • #333 for the h1, h2 and section link colors
  • use approximate, relative font sizes
  • I used a border-top on the nav li
  • I used a border-bottom on the last li by using nav li:last-child
  • I created my nav transition and hover on the li, not the a tag
  • I changed the background color and padding to get the hover effect, the darker background color was #496B87
  • I coded the first two nav links for you so you can see how I did it. I also coded the back to top links.
